How to book a classroom?

Just write an email to booking@cuhk.edu.cn, including the date, time, and usage in the body.

How to change my password?

If you are in campus or using VPN, click Change CUHK(SZ) Password.

Change CUHK(SZ) Password if you are not in campus.

Please be noted that, you need to change your password every 90 days, otherwises you will be unable to access your CUHK(SZ) account until the password has been reset.
The password should not be the same as passwords used last 3 times.
At least 8 characters long, and the following 4 types of characters should be included.

  • Uppercase letters (A, B, C, ...)
  • Lowercase letters (a, b, c, ...)
  • Numbers (1,2,3,.....)
  • Special characters (! @, #,...)
